ABOUT COMPANY

KGC Private Limited

KGC Private Limited is one of India's leading Engineering & Advisory Services consulting firms. Since its establishment in 2008, the company has been delivering result-oriented consultancy services across a wide range of industries.

We specialize in post-harvest processing, industrial infrastructure development, and end-to-end project consultancy for sectors including food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, chemicals, FMCG, agro-processing, marine, dairy, meat processing, and general manufacturing industries. Our expertise extends beyond food to cover diverse industrial domains requiring advanced processing and packaging solutions.

As recipients of several prestigious awards in the processing and packaging industry, we offer a complete range of consulting services, from conceptualization and project planning to execution and commissioning.

Our team of highly qualified professionals brings extensive experience in technologies such as extrusion, dehydration, pulping, concentration, retort processing, freezing, and advanced manufacturing systems. We also provide technical advisory, plant setup, process optimization, system development, and turnkey project solutions across multiple industries.

In addition, we act as a Regulatory and Compliance Consultant, assisting clients in meeting industry standards, certifications, and protocols across regions including Gujarat, Mumbai, and Kolkata.

KGC Private Limited is recognized as a trusted consultant for juice, beverage, dairy, snacks, spices, API (pharmaceutical), honey, chemical processing, and industrial manufacturing projects. We support clients with government subsidy coordination and approvals through central and state-level schemes, ensuring smooth project execution and financial optimization.

At KGC Private Limited, we are committed to delivering innovative, efficient, and scalable consulting solutions tailored to the evolving needs of modern industries.
